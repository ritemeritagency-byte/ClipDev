What Collaboration Means at ClipDev

ClipDev collaboration is a shared-learning program for real digital projects. It is built for aspiring developers, designers, and creators who want practical field experience.

Project compensation depends on each client's available budget. Collaborators are not ClipDev employees. You join as a collaborator, build your portfolio, and grow with the community.

  • Website design and landing page development
  • Graphics design for digital campaigns
  • Social media content creation support
  • Social media manager collaboration tracks
  • Real project workflow with mentorship support
  • Opportunity to become a lead contributor on suitable projects
