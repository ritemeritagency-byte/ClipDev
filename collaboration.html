What Becoming a Talent Means at ClipDevs

The ClipDevs talent program is a shared-learning path for real digital projects. It is built for aspiring developers, designers, and creators who want practical field experience.

Project compensation depends on each client's available budget. Talents are not ClipDevs employees. You join as community talent, build your portfolio, and grow with the team.

  • Website design and landing page development
  • Graphics design for digital campaigns
  • Social media content creation support
  • Social media manager talent tracks
  • Real project workflow with mentorship support
  • Opportunity to become a lead contributor on suitable projects

Before You Apply

Talent Program Policy

  • Project compensation depends on each client's available budget.
  • Talents are not employees under ClipDevs.
  • Scope includes websites, graphics, social content, and social media support.
  • This is a shared-learning environment with team accountability.
  • Main goal: real-world experience, portfolio growth, and community impact.

Talent Engagement Terms

  1. Talents are contacted only when their skills match a project.
  2. This is project-based collaboration, not daily fixed work.
  3. Payment is provided when ClipDevs assigns work or connects talent to a client project.
  4. Talents can manage their own schedule as long as deadlines and quality standards are met.
